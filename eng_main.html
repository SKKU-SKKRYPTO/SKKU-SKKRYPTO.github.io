SKKRYPTO is an blockchain society
based on Sungkyunkwan University.
We the fair and free WEB 3.0 world through
in-depth research and development of blockchain technology.
Started in 2018, SKKRYPTO continues journey
even after the COVID-19 pandemic and Crypto Winter.

Activities

Research Team

Based on a basic understanding of the blockchain ecosystem, the research team investigates market trends and conducts case studies on key products.
Junior session starts with analysis of Bitcoin and Ethereum white papers and brings deep insights into Web3 including DeFi and Crosschain.
In the senior session, we mainly conduct team presentations, and we write and share a column for each presentation.
In addition, we are continuously conducting industry-academic cooperation with various blockchain companies such as Xangle and NEAR.

Dev Team

Dev team studies the languages and frameworks necessary for developing blockchain smart contracts and dApps, including Solidity, Rust, and Web3.js.
Junior session covers the basic grammar of Solidity and practices deploying contracts in the Remix local environment.
After, in senior sessions, the Toy Project provides team experience and full-fledged program planning and development begins.
Now we are processing Web3 services such as payment services, and social communities as an industry-academic cooperation project.

FAQ

Q. Is SKKRYPTO an society for investment in cryptocurrency?

A. That's not true. There are also academic members who act as professional investors, but all academic members work with one goal of popularizing blockchain.

Q. For applying, do I need knowledge on blockchain and development skills?

A. It would be nice if you have background knowledge about blockchain or are already good at developing it, but it's okay if you don't. SKKRYPTO provides a basic curriculum for beginners,
so you don't have to feel pressured just because you don't know Web3.

Q. What can I learn and gain from scripto activities?

A. You can study blockchain in depth, which was not covered in detail in colledge or in the media. In addition, you can gain industry-academic cooperation and networking experience
with blockchain companies such as Hashed and Blockchain Labs.

Q. Is there anything I need to have to apply for?

A. Regardless of academic background, affiliation, or age! Anyone who has a lot of enthusiasm for blockchain and can consistently attend weekly sessions is welcome.
